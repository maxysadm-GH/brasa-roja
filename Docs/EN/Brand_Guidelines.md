# 1. BRAND OVERVIEW

## Brand Story
**Brasa Roja** embodies the warmth of family tradition and the art of fire-cooked cuisine. The name translates to "Red Ember" - representing the glowing heart of our cooking fires that have brought families together for generations.

## Brand Values
- **TradiciÃ³n** (Tradition) - Authentic recipes passed down through generations
- **Familia** (Family) - Every guest is family
- **Fuego** (Fire) - The passion and craft of open-flame cooking
- **Calidad** (Quality) - Only the finest ingredients

## Brand Personality
Warm, Authentic, Inviting, Premium yet Accessible, Family-Oriented

# 3. LOGO VARIATIONS

## Secondary Logo (Horizontal)
For horizontal spaces where vertical stacking doesn't fit.
- Icon to the left, wordmark to the right
- Use for letterheads, email signatures, website footers

## Wordmark Only
Text-only version without the flame icon.
- Use when the icon has already established brand presence nearby
- Minimum width: 1 inch (25mm) print / 100px digital

## Icon Mark (Flame Only)
The flame with swirl and embers, without text.
- Use for: Profile photos, favicons, app icons, embroidery on small items
- Minimum size: 0.5 inches (12mm) / 48px
- Social media avatars, uniform embroidery patches

## Monochrome Versions

### Gold Monochrome
- All elements in Brasa Gold (#C9882B)
- Use on dark backgrounds or when single-color printing is required

### White Monochrome
- All elements in white (#FFFFFF)
- Use on dark or colored backgrounds

### Black Monochrome
- All elements in rich black
- Use for fax, newspaper ads, single-color print

# 6. TYPOGRAPHY

## Primary Font: Display/Headlines
**Cinzel** or **Trajan Pro**
- Use for: "BRASAROJA" wordmark recreation, headlines, signage
- Weight: Bold/Semibold
- Character: Classic, premium, timeless

## Secondary Font: Body Text
**Lora** or **Libre Baskerville**
- Use for: Menu descriptions, body copy, marketing materials
- Weight: Regular, Italic for emphasis
- Character: Readable, elegant, warm

## Accent Font: Tagline
**Parisienne** or **Great Vibes**
- Use for: "TradiciÃ³n Familiar" tagline only
- Character: Script, handwritten feel, personal

## Font Hierarchy
1. **Headlines**: Cinzel Bold, ALL CAPS, tracking +50
2. **Subheads**: Cinzel Semibold, Title Case, tracking +25
3. **Body**: Lora Regular, 10-12pt, line height 1.5
4. **Captions**: Lora Italic, 8-10pt

# 7. PRINT SPECIFICATIONS

## Signage

### Outdoor Signage (Illuminated)
- **Material**: Aluminum composite with LED backlighting
- **Vinyl**: 3M 3630-43 Tomato Red Translucent (flame areas)
- **Vinyl**: 3M 3630-25 Sunflower Translucent (gold areas)
- **Resolution**: Vector at final size
- **Minimum size**: 24" width for readability at 50+ feet

### Window Decals
- **Vinyl**: 3M 680CR-72 Ruby Red Reflective
- **Vinyl**: 3M 180mC-12 Gold Metallic
- **Application**: Inside glass, facing out

### Menu Boards
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI at print size
- **Color Mode**: CMYK
- **Format**: PDF/X-1a or AI with outlined fonts

## Uniform Embroidery

### Thread Specifications (Madeira Polyneon)
| Color | Madeira Code | Alternative (Isacord) |
|-------|--------------|----------------------|
| Brasa Gold | #1725 (Gold) | #0821 |
| Roja Red | #1839 (Paprika) | #1913 |
| Deep Ember | #1674 (Rust) | #1355 |
| White | #1801 | #0010 |
| Black | #1800 | #0020 |

### Embroidery Sizes
- **Polo/Shirt Chest**: 3.5" wide (left chest)
- **Apron Center**: 5" wide
- **Cap Front**: 2.5" wide (icon only recommended)
- **Jacket Back**: 8-10" wide (full logo)

### Embroidery Notes
- Use icon-only mark for items under 2" width
- Remove fine ember particles for sizes under 3"
- Simplified flame (no gradient) for best stitch quality

## Screen Printing

### Ink Specifications (Pantone Matching)

### Print Size Guidelines
- **T-shirt Front**: 10-12" wide centered
- **T-shirt Back**: 12-14" wide centered
- **Apron Front**: 6-8" wide

## Print Production Notes
- All stickers should be exported at 300dpi minimum
- Include 0.125" bleed for cut tolerance
- Die-cut stickers: use transparent background PNG
- Standard stickers: add background color matching brand palette
- CMYK color mode for professional printing
