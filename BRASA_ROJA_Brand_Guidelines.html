Brand Story

Brasa Roja embodies the warmth of family tradition and the art of fire-cooked cuisine. The name translates to "Red Ember" - representing the glowing heart of our cooking fires that have brought families together for generations.

Brand Values

  • Tradición - Authentic recipes passed down through generations
  • Familia - Every guest is family
  • Fuego - The passion and craft of open-flame cooking
  • Calidad - Only the finest ingredients

Logo Symbolism

  • Flame: Passion, warmth, cooking tradition
  • Swirl: Movement, gathering, community
  • Embers: Rising quality, celebration
  • Colors: Fire (red), Gold (premium), Warmth

Embroidery Notes

  • Use Icon Mark only for items under 2" width
  • Remove fine ember particles for sizes under 3"
  • Use simplified flame (no gradient) for best stitch quality
  • White thread on dark fabrics, Charcoal on light fabrics
Brand Guidelines BRASA ROJA | 10

FILE DELIVERY

Provide these file formats to vendors and partners.

Vector Files (Required)

  • AI - Adobe Illustrator (editable)
  • EPS - Universal vector format
  • SVG - Web applications
  • PDF - Print-ready, outlined fonts

Raster Files (Support)

  • PNG - Transparent, 300 DPI
  • JPG - Solid backgrounds, 300 DPI
  • TIFF - Archival quality

Specialized Files

  • DST/PES - Embroidery machines
  • DXF - CNC/laser cutting

Quality Standards

  • Print: 300 DPI minimum
  • Large signage: 150 DPI acceptable
  • Web: 72 DPI, RGB color
  • Always provide CMYK for print
