Brand Story & Values

Brasa Roja embodies the warmth of family tradition and the art of fire-cooked cuisine. The name translates to "Red Ember" — representing the glowing heart of our cooking fires that have brought families together for generations.

Our brand stands at the intersection of authentic tradition and modern excellence. Every flame tells a story, every dish carries the legacy of recipes perfected through decades of passion and dedication.

We believe in the transformative power of fire — how it brings raw ingredients to life, how it gathers people around the table, and how it creates moments that become cherished memories.

Tradición

Authentic recipes passed down through generations, preserving the soul of our cuisine.

Familia

Every guest becomes family the moment they walk through our doors.

Fuego

The passion and craft of open-flame cooking that defines our identity.

Calidad

Only the finest ingredients, prepared with unwavering attention to quality.

Brand Personality

Warm, Authentic, Inviting, Premium yet Accessible, Family-Oriented. Our visual identity should always reflect these core attributes, creating a consistent experience across all touchpoints.

Primary Logo

Logo Construction

The primary logo consists of three harmonious elements working together:

  • Icon Mark — Stylized flame with swirling base and rising embers
  • Wordmark — "BRASAROJA" with "BRASA" in gold and "ROJA" in red
  • Tagline — "Tradición Familiar" in elegant script

On Light Backgrounds

On Dark Backgrounds

Clear Space Requirements

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the letter "A" in BRASA around all sides of the logo.

Minimum Size

Print: Minimum width 1.5 inches (38mm)
Digital: Minimum width 150 pixels
Below these sizes, use the Icon Mark only.

Gold Visibility on Light Backgrounds

The Brasa Gold (#C9882B) has a contrast ratio of 3.2:1 on white backgrounds. To ensure optimal visibility, use one of these approved display methods:

  • Dark Container — Place logo in charcoal (#2D2D2D) box on white/light backgrounds. Recommended for maximum impact.
  • Cream Background — Use Crema (#F5EFE0) instead of pure white for improved contrast. Most on-brand option.
  • Drop Shadow — Apply subtle CSS shadow: filter: drop-shadow(0 4px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.15))
  • Enhanced Contrast — Boost display: filter: contrast(1.1) saturate(1.15)

See Logo_Display_Options.html for visual comparison of all options.
